Unparalleled Strength

Saitama ossessesseemingly limitless physical strength and is capable of effortlessly defeating powerful monsters and villains with a single, nonchalant punch (although this strength is not limited to punches). Saitama's punch is so powerful that many enemies simply explode when struck. He is also shown to be able to effortlessly destroy walls and buildings. An example of this casual, yet overwhelming offensive power occurs when Saitama launches himself from the top of a building, blasts right through a giant meteor with a punch, lands safely, and just calmly decides to go home.[43] Another instance is when Sonic tries to slice Saitama's head in half with a sword, but Saitama stops the sword by catching it with his teeth, effortlessly shattering the blade with the power of his jaw.[44] When launched from Earth to the moon by a strike from Boros, he used his jumping power to return to Earth in mere moments, causing a huge shock wave and immense tremors on the surface of the moon upon departure, as well as on Boros's ship upon landing.[45] A "normal" punch from him was shown to be very fatal, even to someone as durable as Boros.[46] A single "serious" punch from him was able to counter Boros's strongest attack, Collapsing Star Roaring Cannon, an attack capable of wiping out the entire Earth's surface, Saitama completely negated the attack using his serious punch, the shockwave from the punch was also powerful enough to part clouds on a global scale.[47] Saitama is capable of destroying the Earth if he wanted to.[48] His strength has been said by numerous characters to defy reason.

Enhanced Leap

Saitama's inability to fly is somewhat compensated by his immense leg strength, allowing him to leap tremendous heights and distances at high speeds. This can be seen when Saitama smashed his head through the ceiling of a gymnasium during the hero tryouts simply by jumping. He was also able to smash a building into its foundation just by leaping off from its roof.His greatest feat in this regard, however, was when he leaped from the surface of the moon and ended up back on Earth at a specified point, leaving a crater on the moon's surface in the process. He is also able to jump while still in the air, adjusting his angle of ascent or descent thanks to his amazing power.

Unparalleled Speed and Reflexes

Saitama was able to complete a 1500 meter dash in an instant during his hero tryouts.[58] He has proven able to effortlessly keep up with Speed-o'-Sound Sonic, who can move at above hypersonic speeds.[59] Even Genos's computerized targeting systems have difficulty tracking him.[60] Saitama once bit Sonic's sword at such great speed, Sonic didn't even know what happened.[61] Saitama has also shown the ability to run down a building to save a piece of salmon he was having for lunch, and leap back up said building without bystanders noticing he had even done so.[62] His speed is so impressive that he can even outpace the likes of Flashy Flash, easily dodging his sword swings.[63] When kicked to the moon by Boros's attack, Saitama returned to Earth in mere seconds which shows that Saitama can even travel at sub-relativistic speeds. Saitama also easily blocked Geryuganshoop's near lightspeed rocks, the speed of which being like a bad joke to him.[64] Ironically, despite his incredible reflexes and speed, he still has problems squashing a simple mosquito.
